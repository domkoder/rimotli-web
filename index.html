Listen.

We take the time to listen to understand your pain points

It’s our understanding that the solutions we build are not about our technology at all. They are about your problems---fixing them.

That’s why the first thing we look forward to doing is talk to you about these issues. We want to understand how they affect your business, what are your current methods for getting around them and what a perfect world devoid of these pain points would look like.

Strategize.

Develop strategies that deliver results

Working very closely with you, we develop strategies that take into account every point made at the first stage.

We deliver schematics, wireframes and more material to help you visualize clearly what the building process will entail.

You approve and we are good to go.

Build.

We code

This is the fun part.

We use the most appropriate stack for your project. We build using clean, managable and scalable code.

And we make it just plain awesome

Test.


Great job! We have your solution and she’s a looker.

We are not hands-off quite yet. At this point we most likely have a great software that’s not quite solving all of the problems yet.

Here, we’ll try our best to break what we just built. and we’ll succeed.

Iterate.

We fix what we broke. Now it’s much stronger

Our tests will usually reveal certain issues such as bugs, imperfect executions, etc. This is normal afterall, no software is perfect on it’s first build.

We’ll identify these issues, fix them and keep on fixing them. The iteration stage never ends as improvements to the software will (should) be an on-going exercise.

We can be your partners constantly growing your solution as your company grows or we could train your staff to take over the reigns.
